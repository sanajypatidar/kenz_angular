STEP 1 : CONSULTATION/DESIGN

We work with our clients and design to create employee apparel that perfectly compliments the environment and customers brand. This includes a detailed storyboard to help our clients see how our designs enhance their vision.

STEP 2 : Samples

Once we are given the green light, we cut the patterns then make the samples. We do this in house here at Kenz. The finished samples are then presented to the client.

STEP 3 : Manufacturing

Once the samples are accepted, we begin the manufacturing. Turn around time is swift for most orders. Extremely large orders or uniquely detailed orders may require more time.

STEP 4 : Reorders

Our relationship with our clients does not stop upon delivery. We are available and always prepared to reorder no matter what and it’s over easier to order using our software
